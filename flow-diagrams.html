Three option diagram

  1. You were employed full-time or part-time under the EBA for the relevant year.
    1. You were still employed under the EBA at calculation date.
      1. You are eligible for the COLA payment. No further action required. COLA payment will be automatically processed.
    2. You changed agencies and are no longer employed under the same EBA at calculation date.
      1. You were a Queensland Government employee at EBA calculation date.
        1. You are eligible for the COLA payment. You must complete the COLA form to receive payment.
      2. You were not a Queensland Government employee at EBA calculation date.
        1. You are not eligible for COLA payment.
    3. You were never employed by Queensland Government.
      1. You are not eligible for COLA payment.
  1. You were casually employed under the EBA for the relevant year.
    1. You were still employed under the same EBA at calculation date.
      1. You worked under the same EBA in the 12 weeks leading to the calculation date.
        1. You are eligible for the COLA payment. No further action required. COLA payment will be automatically processed.
      2. You did not work under the same EBA in the 12 weeks leading to the calculation date.
        1. You are not eligible for COLA payment.
    2. You changed agencies and are no longer employed under the same EBA at calculation date.
      1. You remained a Queensland Government employee and worked within 12 weeks leading up to the calculation date.
        1. You are eligible for the COLA payment. You must complete the COLA form to receive payment.
      2. You were not a Queensland Government employee at EBA or you did not work within 12 weeks leading up to the calculation date.
        1. You are not eligible for COLA payment.
    3. You were never employed by Queensland Government.
      1. Some other option
      2. Another option
