  1. Is there demand or need for information on this topic?
    1. No
      1. Consider an alternative communication method
    2. Yes
      1. Is the information covered in an existing article?
        1. No
          1. Is the information time-sensitive or subject to frequent-changes?
            1. Yes
            2. No
              1. Is the information broadly relevant or limited?
                1. Limited
                2. Broad
                  1. Seek approval to proceed
        2. Yes
          1. Is the information accurate and current in the existing article?
            1. Yes
              1. New article not required
            2. No
              1. Can the information be added to the existing article? e.g. and exception
                1. Yes
                  1. Update existing article
                2. No
                  1. Consider updating the existing article so it connects with/supports the new article
  2. Does the information have...
    1. Instructions to follow
      1. Create a new article using the 'How to' template
    2. A question to answer
      1. Create a new article using the 'FAQ' template
    3. Generic in nature e.g. definitions, reference material
      1. Create a new article using the 'Standard' template
